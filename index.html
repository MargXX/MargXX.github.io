Bare-Metal Driver Stack in C

In Progress

Bare-metal C peripheral drivers for the RP2040: GPIO, SysTick, UART and I2C complete, BMP390 in progress. No HAL.

A peripheral and device driver library written directly against the RP2040 datasheet, with no vendor HAL. GPIO, SysTick-based timing, I2C, and UART drivers are complete and validated on hardware using OpenOCD and CMSIS-DAP. BMP390 is in active development. Planned next: SPI and device drivers for the LSM9DS1 IMU and W25Q128 flash for onboard logging. Register definitions are isolated from driver logic to keep the public API clean and the implementation portable to STM32 Cortex-M.

Boba Machine

In Progress

CAD · 3D Printing · Mechanical Design · Systems Integration

Collaborative build of an automated boba dispenser with Wi-Fi ordering and a learned recipe optimizer. My contribution is the mechanical side: CAD of the dispensing mechanism and chassis, 3D-printed parts, and integration of the servo-actuated valves with the overall assembly. Firmware and the gradient descent optimizer are my collaborators' work. The system takes orders from a web interface, dispenses by holding servo-controlled valves open for calibrated durations, and learns from 1–5 ratings to refine ingredient ratios over time.
